Frequently asked questions

My game crashed/freezed/bugged?

Send a email to help.senapp@gmail.com with a description of what you were doing, where you were when the crash. If keeps happening try to record it and send the video with the mail. The more details the better!

Please dont send a mail like "The game crashed" "The game has bugs". These messages don't help at all, this is like saying "There are problems in the world" please be specific about when, where and how your problem happend, to your best knowledge.

Can you make a club simulator as well?

Yes!

I will start working on country specfic versions when I fell International Football Simulator is at a very good level (I don't think it is to far away).

I bought no ads but I still get ads?

When you launch the game, go to the settings menu and click "Restore Purchases". If this doesn't fix the issue then send a email to help.senapp@gmail.com with your recipt number so I can refund you. Example: GPA.3213.3123.1231
